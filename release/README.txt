\f0\fs24 \cf0 - How to Install:\
\
If you have Flash Player installed simply double click on the .swf file to play, if you don't,  use your browser (preferable Chrome or Fire Fox), simply drop the .swf in a new tab to start the game.\
\
Beat Flight is made with Flixel, sin this folder is included all the game assets and code needed (Flixel included with Flixel Power Tools) to compile and make your own build of the game. Simply make a new project in Flash Builder add the files and build.\
\
- Concept: \
\
In Beat Flight you are a space pilot who's heart is failing, you continually get worse until your heart stops. You have to collect med packs to keep your heart rate up but you can also blow your heart out if you take too much so you must keep your heart rate balanced and survive as long as you can. \
\
- How to play:\
\
Hold X+C to start the game, then use the UP and DOWN arrow keys to grab or avoid the incoming med packs.\
